Privacy Policy

The purpose of this privacy policy is to help you understand how we gather, use, and manage our customer’s information.  By using Snowball and other Squanda products you consent to collection and processing of your data in accordance with this policy as well as the terms of use.

More generally, we think of our privacy policy as a feature and hope that you’ll agree.  

What data do we collect?

Snowball brings all of your conversations together: it’s a single place for all your messages from all of your apps.  In order to provide this service we access, collect, and organize the following information:

  1. The list of messaging apps you use.
  1. Incoming notifications and related metadata from these messaging services.
  2. Profile photos, email addresses, and names from your phone’s contact database.
  3. The messaging apps you use and how often you are using them.

We use this information to provide the Snowball service.

Snowball is an Android service, which means that it’s running and collecting data from the moment it’s first started until it’s uninstalled from your device.

Notification Access

Snowball functions by retrieving notifications from your system notification tray and re-organizing them in the Snowball interface.  For this to work we request that you opt into Notification Access (accessible under Settings > Security > Notification access).  The product will not function without this permission granted.

Note that you may disable this permission at any time, though Snowball will cease to function.

Third Party Services

Our policy does not cover third party services.  For Snowball, these services include Hockeyapp and Mixpanel.  To learn about their practices please visit their policies.

Android Permissions

We request the following android permissions for the following reasons:

  1. android.permission.READ_CONTACTS: We use email addresses, and names from your phone’s contact database to find a suitable photo for a message if an incoming notification does not provide one.
  2. android.permission.RECEIVE_SMS: We load your SMS messages into Snowball using this permission in a more reliable way than would be possible from only reading notifications.
  3. android.permission.DISABLE_KEYGUARD: We use this permission to link directly to conversations from the lockscreen.
  4. android.permission.GET_TASKS: We detect what apps you are using at a given time to correctly set the unread state for a message in Snowball.  If you’ve visited an app since received a message in Snowball we set all messages from that app to the READ state.
  5. android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE: We use this permission to detect when you’re on the phone and auto-hide the Snowball tab.
  6. android.permission.SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: We use this permission to show incoming messages on your lockscreen.
  7. android.permission.RECEIVE_MMS: We load your MMS messages into Snowball using this permission.
